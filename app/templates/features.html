Features

Everything you need to run your fleet

Purpose-built for South African taxi operators. No complexity, just the tools that matter.

Income Tracking

Log daily earnings per taxi in seconds. One entry per taxi per day with driver attribution.

Fuel Management

Record every fuel purchase with litres, cost, and odometer readings. Know your fuel efficiency.

Breakdown Tracking

Log breakdowns with timestamps, reasons, and parts used. Track downtime costs automatically.

Driver Management

Manage drivers, assign them to taxis, track performance and idle days across your fleet.

Fleet Reports

Income summaries, taxi-based reporting, depot-based reporting, driver performance, and route-based reporting — all in one place.

Route-Based Reporting

Tag income entries to routes and see which routes are profitable. Make data-driven route decisions.

Role-Based Access

Owners control everything. Managers can log data. Your data stays isolated and secure per organisation.

Works Offline

Entries queue locally when you're off-grid and sync automatically when connectivity returns.

Secure by Design

JWT-based authentication with bcrypt password hashing. Multi-tenant isolation built from day one.
