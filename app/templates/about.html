About Us

Built for South African roads

We started TaxiOps because fleet owners deserve better than spreadsheets and paper logs.

Our Mission

Every day, thousands of taxi operators across South Africa manage their fleets with handwritten notes, paper receipts, and mental arithmetic. Income gets lost, fuel costs go untracked, and breakdowns eat into profits without anyone noticing until it's too late.

TaxiOps changes that. We built a mobile-first platform that works on any phone, with or without internet, designed specifically for the South African taxi industry. No complicated software. No training required. Just the tools you need to run your fleet profitably.

Simple

Log income in under 20 seconds. No training needed.

Secure

Your fleet data is isolated and encrypted. Role-based access for your team.

Offline-First

Works without internet. Syncs automatically when you're back online.
